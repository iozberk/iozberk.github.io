November 2025

AI in Education Summit

Exploring the Future of Artificial Intelligence in Learning, Teaching, and Organizational Transformation.

📅 November 2025 📍 Boston, MA
Overview

What the summit explored

AI for Organizational Efficiency

AI's role in operations, marketing, finance, admissions, and institutional scaling.

AI Literacy & Ready Workforce

Ensuring students graduate with the essential AI competencies required for their future careers.

AI in Teaching & Learning

Using AI-powered tools to enhance pedagogy and personalize student learning paths.

The Future of AI in Ed

Predicting emerging trends and preparing educational institutions for what comes next.

Program Format

One day, two halves

🌅 Morning Conference

9:00 AM - 12:30 PM: Keynotes, lightning talks, and fireside chats featuring university leaders, policymakers, investors, and AI experts.

🌆 Afternoon Workshops

1:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Immersive, hands-on sessions including the Agentic AI Deep Dive with Harvard Data Science Review and AI Use Cases/Tools.

Afternoon Workshops

Hands-on, immersive sessions

Agentic AI Deep Dive for Educators

Led by Xiao-Li Meng (Founding Editor, Harvard Data Science Review) and Sam Paddock (CEO, Next Gen Learning). Explore how AI agents can transform educational practice. Features the agentic AI tutor, Paski, achieving an NPS of +78 for its inaugural cohort.

AI Use Cases and Tools for Education

Moves beyond basic prompt engineering to introduce a more nuanced and intentional approach to AI collaboration.
