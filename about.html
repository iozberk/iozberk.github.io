About AIxEd

A driving platform for AI in education

Bringing the AI-in-education community together to make sense of how artificial intelligence is reshaping learning, teaching, and the workforce pipeline.

Our Mission

AIxEd is a dedicated ecosystem designed to navigate the massive shifts brought by artificial intelligence in educational institutions, learning delivery models, and the workforce. We bring together educators, university leaders, AI researchers, policymakers, and industry practitioners to address the most urgent questions of our time: How do we prepare learners for careers in the AI era? How do educational institutions adapt to survive and thrive?

Workforce Summit

Our hallmark annual event focuses on the pipeline from school to career. We explore how industry, academia, and government can collaborate to build data and AI literacy standards, adapt curricula, and upskill the modern workforce.

Strategy Collaborative

An invitation-only, cross-sector working group that meets throughout the year. The Collaborative provides a trusted forum for senior university leaders, AI pioneers, and corporate strategy heads to design and share policies, playbook strategies, and institutional guidelines.

Ecosystem Building

Beyond events, AIxEd operates as a hub for resource sharing, connecting leading educational projects with top industry developers to validate and deploy AI-driven learning tools safely, effectively, and ethically.
